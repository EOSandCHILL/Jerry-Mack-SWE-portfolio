Experience

Marine Corps Veteran, with 12 years of experience in the technology industry, civilian and federal, with 4.5 years of software developer experience professionally, academically, and personally. I am currently a Full Stack Software Developer, with a Public Trust security clearance, developing a data collection and reporting system for the National Science Foundation. My mission is to leverage my strong technical abilities to design, develop and deploy scalable software solutions for government agencies and operations.

My recent acomplishments as a software engineer:

  • In 2023, my team and I began the development of a large-scale data collection software application for The National Science Foundation. We engineered this data collection software system utilizing Typescript, Vue.JS, JSON, Java Spring Boot, Python, PostgreSQL, RESTful API services, Liquibase, Docker, AWS, Bitbucket, Figma, and Jira. The software empowers recipients of The National Science Foundation's grants to effectively manage their awarded funds. Additionally, it enables The National Science Foundation to track grant recipients' progress, facilitating data-driven decision-making for future initiatives.
  • In 2022, I developed software utilizing React, TypeScript, Node.JS, Docker, Jenkins, and AWS that allowed investors to apply for debt using our FinTech app. This feature allowed the company to gain a strategic financial partnership and generate 5% of revenue from approved loans.
