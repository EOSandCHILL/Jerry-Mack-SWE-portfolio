Hello, I'm Jerry Mack!

I'm a Marine Corps Veteran and Software Engineer, who obtained a DoD Secret Security Clearance, during my 5 years as a USMC Sr. Avionics Tech. After the Marines, my interest in advanced aerospace technologies and electrical engineering led me to graduate from Embry Riddle Aeronautical University with my degree in Aeronautics.

During my 8+ years working in the technology industry, both civilian and government, I've analyzed and solved complex problems for software applications, operating systems, and electrical systems utilizing flow charts, logical thinking, and complex problem-solving skills.

As a full-time software engineer I utilize software tools like Jira to manage work, confluence for content management, HubSpot for cloud-based CRM, SharePoint for team documents, Stripe for payment integrations, FullStory for customer UI/UX feedback, AWS to host applications, Figma for wireframing and designing, Docker for running applications, GIT for version control, and GitHub cloud-based repository storage.

During my time as a full-time software engineer and software engineering student, I have gained skills in and experience with HTML5, CSS, SCSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.JS, Solidity, SQL/MySQL, Python, C++, C#, data structures, algorithms, Agile, Scrum, debugging, OOP, UI Design, Mobile Apps, documentation, DevOps, and more.
